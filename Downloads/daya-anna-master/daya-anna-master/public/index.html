Pediatric Rehabilitation

* It is a Multi-disciplinary, collaborative and holistic approach to treat children with various developmental disabilities/ conditions.

* We implement Neurodevelopmental therapy (NDT), Sensory Integration, Play therapy, Cage therapy and many more advanced methods.

* We treat congenital or acquired conditions such as

  • Cerebral palsy
  • Pediatric stroke
  • Birth injuries
    • Erbs palsy
    • Torticolis
    • Syndromes
    • Muscle / Tonal issues
    • Gait abnormalities
    • Spina bifida
    • Muscular Dystrophy
    • Autism and ADHD etc

* We actively interact with children and engage them in various activities during treatment sessions and make them independent as much as possible.
